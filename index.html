Our Mission

Seybold Serves is a Florida-based disaster relief and homeless aid nonprofit dedicated to supporting vulnerable communities through direct humanitarian outreach and resilient emergency communications.

We provide regular food distributions, essential supplies, and disaster preparedness support while also maintaining off-grid communication systems to ensure critical information can still flow when traditional infrastructure fails. Our mission is to show up where help is needed most: feeding people, protecting lives, and strengthening community resilience year-round.

Disaster Relief

Seybold Serves provides emergency food and water distribution during disasters while supporting resilient off-grid communications to help relay critical information when cell service and infrastructure fail.

Homeless Aid

Seybold Serves provides regular food distributions and essential supplies to individuals experiencing homelessness, while connecting them with trusted community partners who offer additional resources and pathways toward stability.
